Our Norway experience had a variety of many different adventures. We saw such diverse regions with different landscapes and experiences.
This web page is divided out into the 6 regions and cities that we visited, in the order that we visted as shown in the map below:

1. Oslo – the capital city, known for its green spaces and museums. We spent 3 days (July 9-11).

2. Sognefjord – nicknamed the King of the Fjords, is the largest and deepest fjord in Norway; 2 days (July 12-13).

3. Bergen -- known as the 'city of seven mountains' and was a Hanseatic trading post during the 13th to 18th centuries ; 3 days (July 14-16).

4. Solstrand – a resort and spa just South of Bergen on the Bjørnafjorden fjord; 2 days.(July 17-18).

5. Andøya Island – 200 miles N. of the Arctic Circle, where my grandparents grew up; 2 days (July 19-20)

6. Lofoten Islands – an archipelago of 6+ islands known for dramatic scenery of mountains and arctic fjords; 3 days (July 21-23)
