Funicular For Views From Fløyen Mountain

Take the funicular railway to Mount Fløyen and see all of Bergen in just 6 minutes. As Wikipedia states... The Fløibanen is a funicular railway in Bergen. It connects the city centre with the mountain of Fløyen, with its mountain walks and magnificent views of the city. It is one of Bergen's major tourist attractions and one of Norway's most visited attractions. The line is 844 m (2,770 ft) long, covers a height difference of 302 m (991 ft). It is a definite must see - but try to avoid the busy tourist times.
