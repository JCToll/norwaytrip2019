Visit With Our Bergen Relatives

Just as with our Oslo experience, our visit with our Bergen relatives was the highlight of our Bergen experience. (Note that I will refrain from using last names for privacy sake.) My mother's cousin, Berit and her husband Erik live in the outskirts of Bergen, in a beautiful new condo. We had previously met Berit's daughter Ingrid and her husband Helge in Oslo, and we also met her brother Gunnar in Oslo. (See the Oslo Relatives page.) Berit and Erik had us over for a lovely dinner, and also invited their son Torgeir, his wife Elizabeth, and their three children, Thea, and sons, ? and ?.
