The Folk Museum

The Folk Museum (Norsk Folkemuseum), or, The Norwegian Museum of Cultural History , shows how people lived in Norway from 1500 to the present through its collections of buildings, farm houses, churches, and artifacts, from around the country.

This is an "open-air" museum with 160 historic buildings. A main attraction is Gol Stave Church from around 1200. The museum focuses on the time period from 1500 until present time, and in-door exhibits feature Norwegian folk costumes, folk art, church art and Sami culture.

This was one of our favorite museums - perhaps because we had sunny warm weather? You can spend a whole day at this museum - or just a few hours as we did.
