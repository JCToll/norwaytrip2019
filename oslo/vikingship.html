Viking Ship Museum

This was one of our favorite museums in Oslo, and I highly recommend it. Just take the small passenger boat from Aker Brygge waterfront to Bygdøy which has 5 museums.

The Viking Ship Museum is located on the Bygdøy peninsula in Oslo, Norway. It houses three of the world's best-preserved Viking era burial ships that were found as part of archaeological finds from Tune, Gokstad, Oseberg and the Borre mound cemetery.

This is a small but worthwhile museum that will only take a short time to visit.
