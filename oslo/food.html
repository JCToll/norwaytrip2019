Our Oslo Food Experience

Not much to say about about eating out in Oslo, because, we stayed at the Thon Hotel, which had a breakfast buffet that was part of the deal. This was the best buffet we have ever experienced - unbelievable! So many of these pictures show the buffet. The hotel also provided a "light dinner", which was perfect. So we only really ate out for lunch in Oslo.

We also had a fantastic dinner at a relative's home, and she served a delicious meal of reindeer meat, shown in the last picture.
