Visit with our Oslo Relatives

My mother has many close relatives living in Norway. The relatives living in Norway that she knows best, are from her father's side, and are mostly cousins that she visited when she was a little girl. One of the highlights of this trip, was to visit her cousins in Oslo, Bergen, and Andoya, and to meet their families.

I will avoid using last names, to keep this more private. So Gunnar, my Mother's youngest cousin, picked us up and drove us to the home of his cousin, Eln Karin and her husband Helge. Eln Karin's father, Andreas, is the twin brother to Gunnar's father, Oliver; Oliver and Andreas are the youngest twin brothers of my Grandpa. At the dinner, in addition to Eln Karin and Helge, was Eln Karin's daughter, Ingrid and her son (Eln Karin's grandson) Olve. She also invited her niece Ingrid and her husband Helge. Yes, there were two Helge's and two Ingrid's - which actually made it easier. So her niece Ingrid, is the daughter of Berit and Erik, which we later met in Bergen.

We had a great time meeting everyone - and also had a delicous meal. Some of the conversation turned to politics -- in general, Norwegians are like Americans - they don't get what the hell happened to the USA and how we elected the president... Couldn't really help them figure this out...
