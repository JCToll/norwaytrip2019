Boat Excursion on the Sognefjord.

We left Balestrand and took the 4 hour boat ride to Bergen. Just when you thought you saw the most beautiful scenery, the next turn down the fjord only got more beautiful. These are just a few of the pictures we took.

At the end of the fjord, where we started our fjord experience, the landscape is composed of tall mountains, narrow fjords, waterfalls, and snowy peaks. As you head towards the entrance to the fjord, the fjord opens up, and the mountains are lower. At the final leg of the trip, after the boat leaves the fjord, the boat travels south along the western coast, weaving around islands through narrow channels.
