Train to Flam - the world'steepest railroad

We took the train from Oslo to Myrdal, and then took the world's steepest rail ride from Myrdal at 2,800 ft/870 m down to Flam, at sea level on the Sognefjord. This is the same itinerary as is done in the well known "Norway in a nutshell" tour. However, we departed from the standard tour, when at Flam we took a ferry to Balestrand, where we spent one day and night. Balestrand is a small quaint fjord town on the Sognefjord. The following day we proceeded by boat to Bergen.
