Oselvar Boating Experience

Solstrand provides rowing boats to go out on the fjord. This type of boat is called an Oselvar, which has been used for centuries on the fjords of Norway. Oselvar boats are very easy to row, and very stable - especially like my boat when there's water sloshing at the bottom which adds ballast weight. So while Carrie was taking in a spa treatment, I went rowing on the Bjornafjorden. It was one of the highlights of the trip. So fun, and easy. Here are some pictures of this adventure.
