Loftr Viking Museum

The Loftr Viking Museum contains a recreation of a Viking Long House and also provides a very good museum about the life and times of the Vikings. It also encompasses an outdoor "live museum" of a small farm, boat building, and a Viking ship. Some tourists comments were a little negative about this museum. We actually thought it was worthwhile - especially because we went on a rainy day.

In addition to the recreation of the "long house", there is a really good museum that shows the typical life of the Vikings, recreated from artifacts found in the area. There were also some really good quick movies. I would recommend this if you are traveling through the area. It's near the town of Borg, about an hour's drive from Svolvaer.
