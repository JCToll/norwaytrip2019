Henningsvaer - the Cutest Fishing Village on the Lofoten Islands

Henningsvaer turned out to be my favorite town in the Lofoten Islands. We can thank Erik for recommending this town. It's only about 20 minutes South of Svolvaer. It's just a well preserved fishing village, that is a little touristy, but in a good way. The next time we return to Norway, I would like to spend more time in the Lofoten Islands, and would make Svolvaer and Henningsvaer the focal point.
