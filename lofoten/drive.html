Driving Through Lofoten Islands

Driving through the Lofoten Island was quite an experience - just ask Carrie. Our marriage survived it... Because we had so many pictures, I decided to add this section. The rain finally caught up with us during our drive through Lofoten, so there aren't as many pictures as I would have liked.

The drive through the islands was amazing. It was all narrow winding roads, sharing no road shoulders with bicyclists, and trying to avoid the large RVs and trucks. Because Lofoten is composed of many small islands, there were many incredible bridges across fjords connecting the islands, and then some crazy long tunnels that bore through the mountains. Several tunnels were about 6 to 8 miles long. One long tunnel actually went down and under a fjord.
