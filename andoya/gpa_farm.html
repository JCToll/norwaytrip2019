Grandpa's Farm & Homestead on Andøya Island

Here are some pictures and a video from Grandpa Hjalmar's homestead, where he was born, and grew up. His family did subsistance farming, and also a lot of fishing in the fjord, which just about 300 yards from the house. It is amazing that his parents, brothers and sisters - in all 11 people, lived in this house. The house is now being lived in by Jan, my mother's cousin, who is retired and takes care of the home. It is amazing how well this home has been preserved, and how little it has changed from when by Grandpa left in the early 1920's. These pictures show more of just the "homestead" and farm house.
