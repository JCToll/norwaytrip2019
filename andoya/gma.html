Grandma's Homestead and Farm

Grandma Agnes grew up on a farm on the Atlantic coast side of Andøya, just south of Noss. The original house has not been lived in for quite some time - although it was recently sold, and will be remodeled. The land next to the home has been retained, and so too has the other farm land, which is farmed by my mother's cousin, Stig, who is from my Grandma Agnes's side of the family. Stig and his wife May have a cafe and farm called Nordtun Gård, where she makes a specialty cheeses.
